Join the City of Florence Chamber of Commerce!

Membership Levels

Non-Profit (NP) Membership

The Non-Profit Membership provides businesses the same benefits as the Gold Membership for the Chamber of Commerce, with that additional benefit that the Non-Profit Membership is free!
FREE

Bronze Membership

The Bronze Membership gets businesses a 10% discount on use of the Chamber of Commerce Gathering Room for any functions they would like to hold, as well as one spotlight on the Chamber of Commerce website per year, chosen at our discretion.
$150 per year

Silver Membership

The Silver Membership provides a 15% discount on use of the Chamber of Commerce Gathering Room for functions, and one spotlight on the Chamber of Commerce website per year, with your choice of what month your business is featured!
$225 per year

Gold Membership

Businesses selecting the Gold Membership with the Chamber of Commerce get use of the Gathering Room at a 25% discount and three spotlights, with the member's choice of what months the spotlight is featured!
$300 per year
