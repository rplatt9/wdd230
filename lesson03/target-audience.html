Target Audience

The target audience for Florence, Arizona's Chamber of Commerce website are those looking for a good place to do business. Everyone know that "location, location, location" is the most important factor in entrepreneurship and we want to show that Florence is it. You might be interested in visiting our website if: you are looking for a place to start your first business, you are a franchisee looking to expand to new locations, you want examples of successful small businesses, and many other reasons.

Personas

Hi, I'm Kevin Fields. I own a couple of manufacturing plants and I am looking for a good location to open a third. It needs to be a place outside a major city but still close enough to major transportation network. It would also need to be a place with plenty of skilled labor that I could hire. I want a place that I can truly invest in. Plus for a location close to outdoor recreation activities that I would enjoy.

Hi, I'm Natali LeGrange. I have spent several years working in the tech industry and am looking for a place to start my own business in data analytics. It would be a small business so I am looking for a cozy place to open up shop. Since I'm just starting out I'd like to be able to get plenty of financial incentives from the place that I end up. I also want a place that is growing and has plenty of new people to the community, like me.

Scenarios

  1. I would like to start a very new business and I am looking for financial incentives.
  2. I am looking for a new location to relocate my business.
  3. I would like to open a new store for my already thriving business.
  4. I am looking for a location in the American Southwest to experiment with a new idea.
  5. I would like to help people in a small, rural community.
